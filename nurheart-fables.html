Nurheart Fables

Nurheart Fables is a gentle, storybook-style video series for children, where timeless values bloom through the adventures of lovable animal friends. Each animated episode brings to life a new illustrated fable featuring characters like Maahir the curious Monkey, Moly Moly the kind Roly Poly, Sami the brave Sparrow, and Hafiz the wise Tortoise. Through tales of patience, sharing, wonder, and discovery, little hearts are invited to journey toward the Secret Garden — a symbol of goodness, growth, and hidden treasure within. With each turn of the page, Nurheart Fables nurtures emotional wisdom, moral imagination, and a spark of light in every child.
