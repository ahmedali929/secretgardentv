Where Your Donation Goes

Your gift helps plant seeds of knowledge, wonder, and faith in young hearts. Every dollar directly supports the creation of engaging, educational programs for children — from the first spark of a story idea to the final frame on screen.

You’re funding:

  • Meaningful storytelling with Qur’anic values at its core.
  • Beautiful animation and illustrations that capture the imagination.
  • Original soundtracks, voiceovers, and post-production that bring each episode to life.
  • Collaboration with experienced educators and creatives to ensure every piece is spiritually rich and developmentally appropriate.

Beyond creating content, your support also:

  • Brings our shows to underserved communities.
  • Maintains our platform and makes it safe for families.
  • Provides free resources for parents and educators around the world.

Transparency matters to us. We use every contribution ethically and efficiently, with one goal in mind: helping children grow in the Garden of Allah’s signs.

Tax Deductible

Secret Garden TV for Kids is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. This means your contributions are not only meaningful — they’re also tax-deductible in the United States.
