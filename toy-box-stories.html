Toy Box Stories

Toy Box Stories is a playful and poetic Islamic show for children. Through animated toys, puzzles, music videos, and stop-motion adventures, each episode opens a door into a new world of meaning—from the Secret Garden Expressway Train to the story of Nabi Nuh’s Ark. Guided by a gentle narrator, children explore timeless Islamic themes and topics like light vs. darkness, being kind when it is hard, the importance of staying clean, becoming who Allah made you to be, and the lives of beloved Prophets (Anbiya). Some toys come to life, others become part of symbolic story worlds—but every episode leads back to the heart, inviting little ones to imagine, reflect, and return to the Garden of Allah (Janna).
