About Secret Garden TV for Kids

Who We Are

Secret Garden TV for Kids is a nonprofit educational organization and creative studio rooted in Islamic values, dedicated to the holistic nurturing of children through spiritually grounded, Prophetically guided media.

We create thoughtful, engaging, shows, games, and educational materials designed especially for children — and their families — to inspire reflection, build character, and connect everyday life with timeless truths.

Our Mission - Be the Mission

Rooted in Islamic understandings of education, our mission is to bring the Garden (al-Jannah) to life for children as a powerful Qur’anic vision and model for living.

We encourage children to embody the lessons they learn, transforming how they see themselves, others, and the world around them. Our goal is to educate a generation of conscious, curious, and compassionate young people by weaving Islamic teachings into joyful, purpose-driven storytelling and media experiences.

Our Vision - Be the Vision

We envision a world where children develop a deep sense of purpose, empathy, and connection to Allah through engaging content that reflects their spiritual, emotional, intellectual, and true selves.

Our work supports parents and educators in raising whole-hearted, thoughtful human beings who live intentionally, love expansively, and learn endlessly.

Our Philosophy — Be the Garden

Our work grows from three roots: the Garden philosophy, which sees all learning as cultivation of the soul back home where we came from; the epistemology of presence (Hudur), which teaches children to know through living fully in the presence of Allah, unlocking Taqwa and the path of God-consciousness; and Ayatology, the Qur’anic art and science of learning through the Ayaat (Signs) of Allah in creation and the self, as a means of acquiring Hikma (wisdom).

Guided by this philosophy, our pedagogy nurtures the whole child — uniting heart, mind, body, and spirit — so that knowledge is not only learned, but lived. Through stories, play, and reflection, children experience the signs of Allah with all their senses and the heart, allowing them to grow into Signs of His beauty and light in the world.

Our Approach in Practice

At Secret Garden TV, education isn’t just about information — it’s about becoming. Inspired by the prophetic model, we nurture human relationships rooted in trust, empathy, and lifelong growth.
Flowing from our three roots — the Garden philosophy, the epistemology of presence, and Ayatology — we shape every story, game, and activity to reflect the way children truly grow. In practice, this means we design content that encourages:
01
Cultivating collaborative learning

Rooted in Garden philosophy, our stories model intergenerational care, where knowledge flows through trust, love, and shared reflection.

02
Encouraging oriented, thoughtful thinking

Flowing from Ayatology, we help children think beyond false dichotomies of science versus religion or faith versus reason, instead using properly grounded Qur’anic signs to strengthen the thinking and discerning capacity of hearts.

03
Connecting systems through ethics

Inspired by the interconnectedness of creation, we guide children to see how ideas, values, and communities fit into Allah’s greater design.

04
Keeping family at the center

Aligned with the Garden philosophy, we affirm home as the first school of the soul, where love and responsibility are learned.

05
Building love-based relationships

Rooted in the epistemology of presence, we foster kindness, loyalty, and forgiveness as living reflections of prophetic character.
