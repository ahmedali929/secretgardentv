About Secret Garden TV for Kids

Who We Are

Secret Garden TV is a nonprofit creative studio rooted in Islamic values and dedicated to nurturing hearts, minds, and souls through thoughtful, engaging, and spiritually grounded media. We create shows, games, and learning content designed especially for children – and their families – to inspire reflection, build character, and connect everyday life with timeless truths.

Our Mission

To grow a generation of conscious, curious, and compassionate children by weaving Islamic teachings into joyful, purpose-driven storytelling and media experiences.

Our Vision

We envision a world where children develop a deep sense of purpose, empathy, and connection to God through engaging content that reflects their spiritual, emotional, and intellectual selves. Our work supports parents and educators in raising whole-hearted, thoughtful human beings who live intentionally and love expansively.

Core Values

Purposeful Creation
Helping children see meaning in the world around them by fostering responsibility, diversity, and creativity as reflections of God's design.
Huzoori (Adab/Akhlaq)
Nurturing character through patience, humility, and presence, fostering noble behavior rooted in Islamic ethics.
Heart: Awakening Consciousness
Encouraging emotional intelligence and spiritual mindfulness through intentional living, dhikr, and empathy-building practices.
Ayatology: Acquiring Hikma
Fostering curiosity, open-mindedness, and a love for learning through deep reflection on the signs of God in life and the world.
The Human is an End in Himself
Teaching children to value every person’s dignity, practice compassion, and nurture collective care over individual gain.
Education as Becoming
Learning is a spiritual journey. We promote mentorship, reflection, and collaborative growth rooted in prophetic teaching models.
Duty Orientation
Instilling a sense of justice, fairness, and accountability, and encouraging responsibility as a core spiritual and civic value.
Love-Based Relationships
Centering kindness, loyalty, empathy, and forgiveness in friendships and community, mirroring the prophetic way.
Wholistic Conceptual Architecture
Encouraging children to think critically and creatively, understanding the interconnectedness of systems, ideas, and values.
Family Orientation
Celebrating family as the foundation of love, responsibility, and belonging, and encouraging children to serve and support their families.
No Dualisms/Binaries
Breaking down simplistic binaries through flexible, analytical, and strategic thinking grounded in Islamic intellectual tradition.
Systems Building: Spiritual Leadership
Equipping children with the skills and vision to lead with ethics, inspiration, and service, following the model of prophetic leadership.
