About Secret Garden TV for Kids

Secret Garden TV for Kids is an entrepreneurial venture aiming to reimagine Islamic education through cutting-edge, appealing, and creative media and entertainment. The website will serve as a central hub for the organization, showcasing its values, projects, and resources while providing an interactive and engaging experience for children and adults.

Contact Us Contact Us
  • What is Secret Garden TV?

    Secret Garden TV is a faith-inspired edutainment platform that offers fun, educational videos for Muslim children. Our content includes martial arts with Islamic heritage, Arabic learning adventures, Quranic duas, and spiritually themed storytelling designed to nurture both hearts and minds.

  • Is the content suitable for all ages?

    Yes! Our content is designed with young children in mind — typically ages 4 to 10 — but many older children and parents enjoy it too. We aim to make Islamic education engaging and accessible for all members of the family.

  • How often do you release new videos?

    We aim to release new episodes regularly, with new content every week or two. Be sure to subscribe to our YouTube channel and join our newsletter so you never miss an update!

  • How can I support Secret Garden TV?

    You can support us by subscribing to our channel, sharing our videos, and donating via the links in our footer. Your support helps us create more high-quality, faith-based content for children around the world.
