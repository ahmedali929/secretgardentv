Frequently Asked Questions

  • A list of the most questions we've been asked, and their answers
  • What is Secret Garden TV?

    Secret Garden TV is a faith-inspired edutainment platform offering fun, educational videos for Muslim children. Our content includes nature and play-based science education, martial arts with Islamic heritage, Arabic learning adventures, Qur’anic duʿas, and spiritually themed storytelling — all designed to nurture the Garden within each child.

  • Is the content suitable for all ages?

    Yes! While our shows are designed especially for ages 4–10, many older children — and even parents — enjoy them too. We make Islamic learning engaging, beautiful, and accessible for the whole family.

  • How often do you release new videos?

    We release new episodes regularly — usually every week or two. Subscribe to our YouTube channel, join our newsletter, and follow our socials so you never miss a new story or adventure.

  • How can I support Secret Garden TV?

    You can support us by subscribing, sharing our videos, and donating via the links in our footer. Your support helps us plant more seeds of faith and learning in children’s hearts around the world.

  • Is your content safe and values-based?

    Absolutely. Every episode is rooted in Islamic values and designed with both faith and child development in mind. We avoid harmful themes, encourage good character, and weave lessons of the Qur’an and Sunnah throughout.

  • How can my children watch your shows?

    Our shows are available on YouTube and can also be accessed through links on our website. We are working toward making them available on more platforms in the future, insha’Allah.

  • How do you approach screen time?

    We believe screen time can be meaningful when used wisely. Our episodes are short, purposeful, and often include hands-on activities, as well as thought and physical exercises, to encourage children to engage with the world beyond the screen.

  • Are the stories based directly on Islamic texts?

    Some stories are inspired by themes from the Qur’an, hadith, and Islamic traditions, while others are original fables that reflect Islamic ethics and worldview. Each show is crafted with spiritual depth and narrative clarity in mind.

Can my child submit artwork, questions, or story ideas?
Yes! We love hearing from our young viewers. You can send drawings, reflections, or story ideas through the contact form on our website. Some may even be featured in future episodes or newsletters (with parent permission, of course).
02
How do you choose the themes for your shows and stories?
We select themes that reflect key Islamic values—like kindness, gratitude, honesty, and community service—while also addressing real-world challenges kids face today. Each theme is carefully aligned with child development principles and Islamic teachings.
06
Is Secret Garden TV only for Muslim families?
While our content is inspired by Islamic values, the lessons—like compassion, responsibility, and honesty—are universal. Families of all backgrounds are welcome and often find our stories deeply enriching.
